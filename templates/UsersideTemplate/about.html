Roots of Community: A Historical Journey

The Municipality of Naawan is a fourth-class municipality in Misamis Oriental with an estimated population of 20,000 as of 2020.

Naawan was recognized as the cleanest town in the Philippines in 2008 and is home to one of the Mindanao State Universities. Located near the ocean, it has a total area of 88.50 kmÂ².

Poblacion, situated at coordinates 8.4327, 124.2919 at an elevation of 10 meters above sea level, is one of the prominent barangays in Naawan. It comprises 12 puroks with a population that grew from 2,724 in 1990 to 5,714 in 2020.
